 How it Works

  • Eligible students must be in good standing with the University and have received federal student loans during the current school year.
  • Panthers Forward participants will have access to an exclusive network of alumni mentors, dedicated to supporting their success.
  • Upon graduation, Panthers Forward participants are asked to pay it forward to future students.
  • Participants are under no contractual obligation to repay Pitt, but their support is important to sustain the program.
  • Donations are tax deductible and directly support future Panthers Forward students.
