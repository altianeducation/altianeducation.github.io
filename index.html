Overview

Student debt has become a national crisis that needs a better solution than lower interest rates. The University of Pittsburgh has committed to a new, innovative method to tackle this crisis, the Panthers Forward Program. The program launches in Fall 2018.

Read More

A Message from Chancellor Gallagher

“It’s not about making an institution bigger, or creating certain foundations or buildings, but it’s about helping other students enjoy what you enjoyed. This could go way beyond just living up to this obligation of yourself. My vision is that this becomes a powerful and positive experience. That this is not just an obligation, this is about creating opportunities."
