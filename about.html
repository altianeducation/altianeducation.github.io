About Panthers Forward

Program Summary

Student debt has become a national crisis that is impacting our community, our university, and especially our students. The Panthers Forward program represents an opportunity for the Pitt community to work together to create a sustainable new model to address this issue. We believe that by investing in our students and working together as a community, we can create a new vision of higher education funding.

Panthers Foward starts with The University investing in its students in the form of $5000 awards that seniors in the 2018-2019 year can apply for. The hope is that the chosen students then pay-it-forward by donating to future years' students when they are able. The goal of the program is to create a sustainable, ever-greening fund that can continually support more students each year through a cycle of giving. With the support of student recipients, current alumni, and community partners, we can move our Panther family Forward.

 How it Works

  • Elligible students can apply for the award for the 2018-2019 academic year. Applications are due September 16th and recipients will be chosen by October 5th.
  • Participants will have the opportunity to attend programs and get to know succesful alumni mentors in the Pittsburgh area.
  • At the end of their senior year, recipients will recieve a $5000 credit that replaces an equivalent amount of federal loans for the academic year.
  • There is no obligation to pay back the award, but recipients will be asked to donate to future years' students after they enter the workforce and are able. Donations are tax deductible and will go directly to future student recipients.
  • Panthers Forward only succeeds through the support of student recipients, current alumni, and other generous donors. By paying-it-forward to the next generation of students, we can grow the program and drastically change the landscape of student debt at Pitt.

Program Highlights


Community

Panthers Forward facilitates student involvement in the Pitt community and helps grow our student-alumni community.

Student Financing

Instead of an interest accruing loan, selected students will recieve a $5000 award to help pay for their senior year.

Financial Flexibility

Because there is no mandatory payments or interest, alumni of the program have more financial flexibility and can donate when they feel they are able.

Mentorship

Program participants will have the opportunity to interact with succesful alumni in the area, and get help in working towards a succesful career.

Paying-it-Forward

Instead of relying on federal or private lenders who try to make money on education financing, Panthers Forward allows direct support of students.

Re-envision Education

Pitt is paving the way to a new vision of higher education, and we need the help of our students, faculty, staff, and alumni to do it. Together we can move to a better future.
