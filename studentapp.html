Student Application

Applications are Due September 17th!

The eligibility criteria to apply to the Panthers Forward Program are below. If you have any questions, please contact us!


  • Graduating in the spring of 2019
  • Current undergraduate student of the University of Pittsburgh, Pittsburgh Campus
  • In good academic standing with the Univeristy
  • Do not currently have a full-tuition or greater scholarship at the University of Pittsburgh
  • Have at least $5,000 in federal student loans for the 2018-2019 academic year
