Overview

Launching in fall 2018, Panthers Forward is a University of Pittsburgh initiative designed to support student success and reduce the burden of student debt.

Read More

A Message from Chancellor Patrick Gallagher

"At the University of Pittsburgh, we shape a proud history of solving seemingly impossible and intractable problems. Panthers Forward—a new model for financing higher education—is bold, unusual and innovative. It's based on a promise to give back and support others. And it's a model that, if successful, could help propel Pitt students to greater financial freedom and lifelong success."
